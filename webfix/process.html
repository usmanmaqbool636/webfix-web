    Idea & Discussion

    It is the little details that are vital to make big things happen. Our initial meeting with you helps us to understand the little details about your project – your concept, business model, purpose, and your market. Our expert strategist will work with you to develop a successful plan that leads us to a winning project.

    Documentation & Use case flowchart

    Once the idea and features are finalize, out BRD team will start working on documentation which include following, Mind mapping, wireframes, use case flow, database flow and build UML diagram and the list of API, so a proper documentation will be build by certified data analyst.

    Designing & Style Guide

    This is the stage when your idea actually starts taking shape. We create wireframe to help you understand the layout of the final product and how users will consume your information. Once we receive approval on the wireframe, we craft user-friendly and intuitive user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) designs.

    Development, Deployment & Testing

    Experienced and talented developers at Source Soft Solutions pair the right programming tools and technologies with the UX/UI designs finalized by the client. From alpha phase, beta phase to testing the product, we keep you updated about the progress during this phase. Thorough testing is done during and post development.

    Launch Of Product

    We go above and beyond in quality assurance and testing. We run a variety of tests on the project to ensure its functionality, usability, consistency, performance, security & other important non-functional traits. Masterpiece is now ready for grand launch! We deploy the final product on respective app stores and web browsers.

    Support Program
